STREAMING

Streaming media is multimedia that is continuously transmitted and consumed from a source, with or no intermediate storage in network components. Streaming refers to the process little of delivering material rather than the content itself. It is a way of transferring or receiving data (particularly video and audio information) as a constant, continuous flow via a computer network, allowing playback to begin while the remainder of the data is still being received.

Like YouTube and other video contents, streaming also have a variety of content available to the viewers. The types of streaming contents will be discussed in the further (next) part of this file. Streams can also be discussed in several platforms, which will also be discussed on the following parts of this paper.

TYPES OF STREAMING CONTENTS

With media streaming, users don’t need to download an entire audio or video file to play it. Instead, the file is sent in a continuous stream of data to the user’s device so that it can be played as soon as it arrives in real-time or near-real time. The user can also pause, rewind, or fast forward the file, as for a downloaded file unless the content is streamed, in which case the user can only watch or participate in content events. Users can stream and use different types of media such as:

• Music
• Videos
• Movies
• TV shows
• Podcasts
• Gaming

TYPES OF STREAMING CONTENTS

What we did is conduct a survey among Grade 9 & 10 Students of APEC Schools - Ortigas Extension, which is our target audience and the focus of our research. 54.8% or 17 students out of 31 total respondents are Grade 9 students, and 45.2% or 14 students are Grade 10 students. 100% or 31 out of 31 total participants are single as for their marital status, all of them are in Junior High School, and they are all Filipino as for their nationality. 27 out of 31 respondents are unemployed, while 4 out of 31 are employed.

Along with those needs, they also need the content to be understandable, interesting, knowledgeable, engaging, catchy, complete, and accurate.
