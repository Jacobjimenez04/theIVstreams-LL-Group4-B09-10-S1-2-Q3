ABOUT

The website would serve as a portfolio as well as a promotional place for our upcoming streaming. The group was originally formed in school year 2022-2023, made for the iPBAs throughout the whole school year. The group consists of 5 members, which are composed of one (1) facilitator, two (2) spokesperson, and two (2) scribes. Specifically, the group have Jacob Gerard Jimenez as Facilitator, Gio Manuel Jaring and Angelo Gabriel Parada as Spokesperson, Avegaille Godoy and Lil Norry De Rivera as our Scribe. The group was named after its group number, which is four (4), then we came up with an idea that we could just use the number four’s romanized version which is IV, that’s how the group came up with the brand name “the IV”.
